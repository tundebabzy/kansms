Help Topics

My message was successfully sent but did not deliver to the person I sent it to.

Delivery of an SMS is dependent on other factors that are beyond our powers like unavailability of network signals in the area where the receipient is located or the receipient's phone might have been switched off for too long. We will only charge for messages that were successfully sent.

Is there any limit on the number of characters I can send in an SMS on this platform

No. However, 160 characters is equal to a page therefore exceeding 160 characters means you will be using more than one unit per receipient for the SMS.

How about if I send more messages than my units can cater for?

In such a case, the number of messages your credit can cater for will be sent. The rest will be not be sent and you will be shown a list of such numbers that were not sent.

Can I transfer my units to another account

Of course you can.

In my sent messages page, what is the difference between 'forward' and 'resend'?

'forward' gives you the option to edit the message and change the receipients but you will be presented with the original message. 'resend' does not allow you edit the message and I will send to all the people the message was originally sent to. There will be a confirmation before I send just in case you change your mind.
